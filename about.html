About us

Health

Our welfare association provides everyday meal and basic medical facilities to the poor and needy to improve their life standard.

Education

Education is the only weapon to eradicate injustice and inequality. It is the basic right of every child. We campaign to provide educational facilities.

Water Management

Our management provides equipment and teaches proper skills and techniques of rainwater harvesting in places devoid of water fit for consumption.

Hackney Foundation is a NGO as well as a non-profit association working for the well-being of people and serving equality in terms of health and education.

Education has no boundaries, the aim of our community is to provide aids for proper education to students coming from every age group. Hackney Foundation works for the betterment of every human.

Regular medical campaigns are held to ensure the sound health of every citizen. Medical kits are provided for free by our organization along with everyday meals to the poverty-stricken.

Many places in the world face a severe water crisis. We provide proper water harvesting equipment and techniques to save the precious resource for later consumption. Water is life, thus we need to conserve it.
